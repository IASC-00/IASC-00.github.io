FAQ

Common questions.

What if my website isn't custom-built?
The chatbot embeds with one line of code. Works on Squarespace, WordPress, Wix, or any site where you can paste a script tag. If you're not sure, I'll check for free on our call.
What happens if the chatbot goes down?
It runs on Render's free tier — 99.9% uptime for low-traffic sites. Since you own the code, you can redeploy anytime, or I can help for a small fee.
Can I update the chatbot myself after delivery?
Yes. The bot is trained from a plain-text file. Changing hours, menu items, or policies is editing a text document — no coding needed.
How is this different from a $30/month chatbot tool?
SaaS tools are generic and subscription-based. After 12 months you've paid $360+ and own nothing. This is built specifically for your business, trained on your content, and yours permanently after one payment.
Do you only work with restaurants?
No — restaurants are a common use case but the same setup works for property managers, salons, dental offices, and most local service businesses. If you handle repetitive customer questions, a chatbot can help.
