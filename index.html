FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need technical knowledge to work with you?
No. I handle everything — design, build, and deployment. You describe the problem in plain English and see it running live before the final invoice.
Where are the tools hosted?
Typically on Render, but tools can run anywhere — your own server, VPS, or cloud provider. You're not locked into anything.
Do you offer support after launch?
Yes — two weeks of support is included with every project. For ongoing maintenance, I offer a monthly retainer starting at $75/mo.
Can the tools connect to software I already use?
Usually yes. Most business tools expose an API or webhook. I can build integrations with most standard business software — CRMs, scheduling tools, email platforms, and more.
What do I actually own at the end?
Everything. Full source code, deployment instructions, and documentation. No subscriptions, no lock-in — the tool is yours to keep, modify, or hand to another developer.
